# General chat scope (anonymous / no medical record)

## What general chat can do

- Explain common symptoms and self-care in broad terms
- Describe red flags that need emergency or facility care
- Encourage healthy habits and prevention

## What general chat cannot do

- Access your saved MediAI profile, medications, allergies, or lab results
- Personalize advice to your history — use **Personalized AI Doctor** after onboarding and an active assistant pass
- Diagnose conditions or prescribe treatment

## If you need personal context

Sign in, complete your health profile and medical questionnaire, and use personalized chat for questions that depend on your medicines, allergies, or chronic conditions.
