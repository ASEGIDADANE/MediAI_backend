# Finding care in Ethiopia (high-level navigation)

## Emergencies

- Life-threatening symptoms: go to the nearest hospital emergency unit or call local emergency numbers if available in your area.
- Do not rely on this app for ambulance dispatch — use local emergency channels.

## Levels of care (general)

- **Health post / health center:** First contact for many illnesses, vaccinations, maternal checks, malaria testing in endemic areas.
- **Primary hospital / general hospital:** More tests, admission, surgery, specialist referral.
- **Specialized centers:** Cancer, cardiac, psychiatric, and other services in larger cities — usually after referral.

## Practical tips

- Bring any medication list, prior test results, and insurance information if you have it.
- If language is a barrier, bring someone who can help interpret or ask for language support at the facility.
- For chronic conditions, keep follow-up appointments even when you feel well.

## What this chat cannot do

- Cannot book appointments, quote prices, or recommend a specific private facility by name for your case.
- Facility lists in the MediAI app locator are for information — confirm services and hours directly.
